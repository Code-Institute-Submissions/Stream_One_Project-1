I'm Still Human

Video about Martin, a homeless man in Dublin, demonstrating the horrendous reality of life on streets. Exposed to diminiscration and acts of violence, to add to his worries. Martin shows resilience in face of his daily struggles and portrays the true side of humanity. If we can keep this in the front of mind and look past the exterior, placing ourselves in his shows, only then may we fully appreciate how tough it is being homeless in Dublin.

Please donate below if you wish the help James, and others like him, through the good work of Action For The Homeless.
